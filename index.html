About Me

I am Swapnaneel Bhattacharyya, a final-year Master of Statistics (M.Stat.) student at the Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata, specializing in Theoretical Statistics. Prior to that, I completed my Bachelor of Statistics (B.Stat.) in 2024 from the same institute.

I have been fortunate to participate in the research internship at the Department of Statistics and Data Science, Carnegie Mellon University (Summer 2025), advised by Dr. Aaditya Ramdas, CMU. Prior to that, I visited the Big Data Summer Institute, School of Public Health, University of Michigan (Summer 2024).

Research Interests

  • Changepoint detection
  • Sequential decision making
  • Causal inference
